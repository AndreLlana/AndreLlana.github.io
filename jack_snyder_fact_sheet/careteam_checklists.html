Care Team Checklists
Morning Shift
    • Wake-up time varies, but no later then 10am.
    • Wake-up routine:
      • CPAP machine (turn off machine, check water level and refill with distilled water to ‘max line’ as needed)
      • Glasses are kept in the tub on his TV tray near his bed – please clean with lens wipes or soap and water
      • Help with clothes change
      • He needs to wear compression socks up to his knee (can wear socks on top) and his Velcro shoes
      • Empty bedside urinals
      • Use toilet
      • At recliner he will need his compression waist belt on (secure it tightly around his mid-section, not too high)
      • Put on headphones for hearing and hat and blanket
      • Recliner is electric so can go up and down using remote attached to the TV tray
    • Breakfast at 10:30 am
    • Blood pressure: There is an automatic cuff on the table near the recliner. Please document blood pressure on the sheet near his recliner.
    • Medication: Morning pills from the container
    • Please have him use the toilet 15 minutes before leaving
    • Please provide water and snacks before leaving
    • Favorites: Cottage cheese with fruit, macaroni salad, apple sauce, hard-boiled egg, jello, pudding, tapioca, smoothies, cereal bar, Ensure drink, diced fruit
Evening Shift
  • Dinner about 6:15 pm
  • Medication: Evening pills from the container around 7:30-8 pm
  • Light cleaning: Wipe counters after dinner, swifter, empty trash as needed
  • Bedtime routine:
    • Toilet
    • Teeth brushing/mouthwash
    • May want 2 puffs of his inhaler if short of breath once he is at his bed
    • In bed:
      • Remove glasses
      • Apply lip balm
      • Fresh water nearby
      • Help with CPAP machine
      • Make sure urinals and walker are close for overnight
    • Sleeps with his bedroom door open
Sundays 
  • Refill medications for the week
  • Wash and change sheets
  • Laundry
  • Charge life alert (at desk)
  • Charge iPad (at desk)
