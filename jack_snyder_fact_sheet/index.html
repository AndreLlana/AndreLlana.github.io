Jackson's History
About
  • He is an old surfer who loved growing up in Southern California and spent his early adult years in Hawaii chasing waves. He has lived in Santa Cruz for over 50 years.

  • He is friendly, but not an over talker as he is very hard of hearing.

  • He has no hearing in his left ear and about 40% in the right ear.

  • He uses an audio amplifier device (with headphones) to amplify the sound of people speaking and the TV.

  • Try to talk loud and slow. He will lip read a bit as well.
Abilities
  • He can get up from his recliner to a stand with his walker on his own (chair has an automatic lift as needed).

  • He can walk to the toilet, his room, kitchen with the walker. He could use reminders to pick up his feet.

  • No lifting should be required, but monitoring for safety and he may need reminders to reach back when sitting down.

  • In bed he can go from a laying down position to sitting at bedside and then standing with the walker.

  • He fatigues easily and sometimes quickly so pay attention to his breathing and legs/feet as he is moving.

  • He can use his albuterol inhaler as needed throughout the day (8 puffs/day).

  • He is a fall risk so please be around to ‘standby’ if he is walking so that you can ask him to sit down if needed.
Activities
  • If he is feeling good energy-wise, he may like to do a little light painting or maybe go outside and do a few minutes of sitting in the sun or watering the plants with the watering can, but he mostly enjoys watching TV.

  • He loves Sophie Dog and she stays near him most of the day.
Frustrations
  • If asked too many questions and he can’t hear, he can get overstimulated and frustrated.

  • If his belongings are moved around the house without communicating where they are moved to.
The House
  • Two other housemates live in the house. Andre is a caregiver tenant (room near bathroom) and Michael is a tenant (room off kitchen).

  • Spare Key: Located at the front of the house under a cement brick near the sitting bench.

  • Please leave the front door unlocked when leaving morning shift (it has a turn style lock from the inside).

  • Garbage night is Sunday for a Monday morning pick-up.

  • Laundry is off of the kitchen and can be utilized after 10 am.

  • Please do not access the garage storage area unless discussed with housemates.
