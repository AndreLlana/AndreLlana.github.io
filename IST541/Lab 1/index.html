How to Setup a CPAP Machine

By Andre Llana


Step 1: Find the Machine

Locate the clients CPAP machine.

It should be next to the bed on the end table.

Link to Video Guide

Step 2: Fill the Humidifier Reservoir

Locate the clear resevoir container and fill to the MAX line with distilled water.      

You will find the distilled water in the supply cabinet. Set aside.  

Do not use tap water.   

Do not insert reservoir into machine before checking power connection.       


Step 3: Attach the Hose

Locate the end of the hose that does not have the mask attached to it. 

Gently, but firmly, guide the end of the hose into the back of the CPAP machine.                  


Step 4: Check Power Cord

On the back of the machine is a place to insert the power cord.    

Make sure that it is plugged all the way in and there is no water under the machine.

Check that there is power and air blowing from the mask.  


Step 5: Check Hose for Leaks, Wear and Tear

While machine is running check for air leaks from the hose and any connecting spots along the hose. 

Check for  mucas and excess fluid. Clean as needed.         

Check that there is power and air blowing from the mask.   

Arrange tubing appropriately for clients use. 


Step 6: Adjust Mask to Clients Needs

Mask should be snug to nose but not tight.    

Signs mask is too tight is the lifting of the nose upward towards the forehead.

Double and triple checking comfort with client is important for a good nights sleep.

(This is highly important with non-verbal clients)
