Diet
FOOD
  • Nancy prefers meals that are low-fat and high-fiber, with a special focus on keeping her cholesterol low. Her meals should be balanced and include fresh fruits and vegetables. For more on heart-healthy diets, visit the American Heart Association.
  • Breakfast favorites:
    • Green smoothie with spinach, kale, and banana
    • Oatmeal with berries and honey
    • Whole wheat toast with avocado spread and a poached egg
  • Lunch favorites:
    • Grilled chicken salad with a light vinaigrette
    • Vegetable soup with quinoa
    • Turkey sandwich on whole grain bread with mustard and lettuce
  • Snack favorites:
    • Almonds and dried cranberries
    • Fresh carrot and cucumber sticks with hummus
    • Low-fat yogurt with granola
  • Dinner favorites:
    • Baked salmon with steamed vegetables
    • Stir-fry with tofu, broccoli, and brown rice
    • Lentil soup with a side of whole grain bread
  • Desserts: Nancy enjoys fruit-based desserts like apple slices with peanut butter or a small serving of dark chocolate. For more on healthy dessert ideas, check out this guide.
  • Food Delivery:
    • Farm Fresh Delivery – Organic produce delivered on Tuesdays
    • Grocery Pick-Up – Rebecca (her neighbor) helps with grocery pick-up on Fridays.
